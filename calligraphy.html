Calligraphy with masters

In our Calligraphy workshop, we have students and tutors researching and studying Chinese Calligraphy in Xi’an Academy of Art. Here you can experience the Chinese Calligraphy Writing with writing brushes, inks, traditional Chinese Xuan Paper and inkstones. Or, if you are interested in Calligraphy and want to do more than one-time experience, you are welcome to attend our regular talks and workshops:

Monday (6pm)

Introduction to Chinese Calligraphy – Things you should know before your start.

Wednesday (7pm)

Calligraphy of Gao Family Mansion – do a “Calligraphy hunting” with our professional volunteers inside Gao Family Mansion, you will find whole loads of amazing Calligraphy works hiding inside anywhere! Then you will do a fun and interesting facsimile practice of your favorite ones under the guidance of our professional groups!

Friday (6pm)

Spring Festival is coming! Do you want to make a unique Couplet in Gao Family Mansion? Here you can find beautiful designed and written couplets in almost every doorstep! Come and be inspired!

Saturday (9pm) & Sunday (4pm)

Fundamental Calligraphy classes for beginners.

Sunday (2pm)

Advanced Calligraphy courses.

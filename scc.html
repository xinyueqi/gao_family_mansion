Student Cultural Centre

The Student Cultural Experience Centre focuses on the traditional art experience including hand crafting, calligraphy practice and photography. We have designed very educational ye interesting projects for these three different themes, allowing school children to satisfy their historical and cultural curiosity, booking required, free of charge.

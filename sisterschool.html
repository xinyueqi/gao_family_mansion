Outreach

Sister Schools

Sister schools is a Gao Fmaily Mansion programme connecting schools in Xi’an with it’s educational program.

Requiring around six hours of class time over a period of six weeks, Sister Schools allows children to participate in a fun and collaborative curriculum based on the amazing cultural heritage of Gao Family’s Mansion. Themes include Chinese heritage, folk art, and Literature and History.

To support participating teachers and school groups, we have a dedicated Sister Schools portal where articles, drawings, photographs, and videos produced by schools are posted and shared.

Since the first school connection took place in Spring 2016, we have established sister school connection with 5 schools in Xi’an, over 400 students from Xi’an and Luo Yang have taken part.

If you are interested in learning more about Sister Schools, please contact Xinyue Qi, Gao Family Mansion education manager.
